Vastu-Cite

A Vastu calculation app, designed, built, and published solo on the Apple App Store.

View on the App Store

Overview

Vastu-Cite takes a traditional Vastu Shastra calculation process — normally worked out by hand or by a consultant — and turns it into a guided, mobile calculation flow. It's the first of my apps to go live on the App Store, published entirely solo.

What it does

Users pick a language, enter the property's measurements and orientation, and Vastu-Cite walks them through the calculation before presenting a clear result screen. The whole flow was designed to feel calm and guided rather than like a raw calculator — appropriate for a subject people take seriously.

Key features

  • Guided calculation flow from input to result, with clean navigation between screens.
  • Multilingual localization with a dedicated language-selection screen, so the app is usable beyond English speakers.
  • Local persistence using UserDefaults and AppStorage to remember user preferences between sessions.
  • Reusable, responsive UI components built entirely in SwiftUI.
  • Published, tested, and maintained on the live App Store — the full release cycle, not just a prototype.
